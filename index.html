Our Meatballs

Classic Chicken Meatballs

A package of Meaty Meaty Meatballs includes 8 large, 100% chicken meatballs - perfect for your furry friend!

All meatballs should be refrigerated immediately upon receipt and will last for about a week in the refrigerator.

You can also freeze our meatballs for up to 6 months. Just make sure you fully defrost them before feeding them to your pet. Meatballs usually take about a day to defrost in the refrigerator.

Our Story

Spoiling Our First Customer

Hi, we're Annabelle and Georgia. We attend Moorpark High School and Campus Canyon. Welcome to our summer business!

The story of our meatballs begins with our dog, Pickles. Pickles was the heart of our family, and her antics always made us smile. In her younger years, Pickles ate just about anything. She loved Goldfish crackers that had fallen to the floor, expensive steaks and roasted chickens left unattended on the kitchen counter, even the occasional late-night Cheeto!

As Pickles got older, her stomach couldn't handle all of the yummy human foods she used to enjoy. Although we kept a steady supply of store-bought treats around, her nose didn’t wiggle with the same delight because they contained so many fillers and chemicals.

Lucky for Pickles, our Mom found the perfect way to spoil her. A few years ago, she made a special batch of our favorite homemade chicken nuggets with JUST the chicken, and Pickles went nuts for them. They became a regular staple in our house. When the COVID-19 pandemic forced us to close down our lemonade stand, we started selling Mom’s meatballs to our dog-owning friends and neighbors. The rest is history!

As you might know, our beloved dog of 15 years, Pickles, passed away in October of 2024. Since then, we’ve been looking at rescues to find a perfect match for the hole our old girl left in our hearts. That sadness has recently been filled with the abundantly snuggly, fluffy, 4-year-old Ginger! Ginger has been the perfect dog so far, filling our lives with love and adoration for her.
