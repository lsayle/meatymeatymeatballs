Our Meatballs

Classic Chicken Meatballs

A package of Meaty Meaty Meatballs includes 8 large, 100% chicken meatballs - perfect for your furry friend!

All meatballs should be refrigerated immediately upon receipt and will last for about a week in the refrigerator.

You can also freeze our meatballs for up to 6 months. Just make sure you fully defrost them before feeding them to your pet. Meatballs usually take about a day to defrost in the refrigerator.

NEW! Turmeric Chicken Meatballs

Our new turmeric chicken meatballs contain 250mg of turmeric per meatball, providing anti-inflammatory and antioxidant benefits. For your dog's safety, vets recommend not exceeding 15-20mg of turmeric per pound of body weight daily.

Our Story

Spoiling Our First Customer

Hi, we're Annabelle and Georgia. We attend Campus Canyon. Welcome to our summer business!

The story of our meatballs begins with our dog, Pickles. Pickles was the heart of our family, and her antics always made us smile. In her younger years, Pickles ate just about anything. She loved Cheerios and goldfish crackers that had fallen to the floor, expensive steaks and roasted chickens left unattended on the kitchen counter, even the occasional late-night Cheeto!

As Pickles got older, her stomach couldn't handle all of the yummy human foods she used to enjoy. Although we kept a steady supply of store-bought treats around, her nose didn’t wiggle with the same delight because they contained so many fillers and chemicals.

Lucky for Pickles, our Mom found the perfect way to spoil her. A few years ago, she made a special batch of our favorite homemade chicken nuggets with JUST the chicken, and Pickles went nuts for them. They became a regular staple in our house. When COVID-19 forced us to close down our lemonade stand, we started selling Mom’s meatballs to our dog-owning friends and neighbors. The rest is history!

We miss Pickles every day, but her legacy lives on in the joy we bring to other dogs with our homemade treats. Every batch we make is a tribute to her—our funny, lovable, and always-hungry best friend.

Helping Our Community

In 2023 we began donating $1 from each package sold to the Moorpark Pantry Plus. We provided over $200 in groceries that year. Earlier this year, we had a sale and raised $400 to donate to the World Central Kitchen, providing aid in Los Angeles to victims of the Eaton and Palisades wildfires. This summer, we will donate 12% of our profits to the World Central Kitchen to support their current projects.

Meaty Meaty Meatballs is not just a way to make summer money anymore, but a chance to provide for communities worldwide and make an impact in areas of need. Our small business is making big differences around the globe, which just shows how a little donation can make a huge impact.
